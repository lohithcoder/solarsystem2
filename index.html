ABOUT SOLAR SYSTEM

Our solar system consists of 1 sun which is a star. It is a part of the Milky Way Galaxy. Our Solar System is about 4.6 billion years old. Around the sun 8 main planets revolve in their particular axis. These 8 Planets are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Some of the planets have their moons that keep revolving around them.
